What is the capitalization rate?
The capitalization rate is a rate that helps in measuring the rate of return on a real estate investment property. It is also known as the cap rate. How to calculate capitalization rate?
To calculate capitalization rate you should use the following formula:
capitalization rate = net operating income / purchase price
example: John has bought a property for $760,000, it has a net operating income worth of $80,000, calculate the capitalization rate calculator?
capitalization rate = net operating income / purchase price
capitalization rate = $80,000 / $760,000
capitalization rate = 10.52%
Why use capitalization rate?
Real estate investors use the capitalization rate because it helps them in evaluating properties and find the best deals in the market.
